## 3. Oversight Procedures  

### **3.1 Pre-Commitment**
- Full DD  
- Fee modeling  
- Stress testing  
- IC approval  

### **3.2 Post-Commitment**
- Quarterly updates  
- K-1 review (if applicable)  
- Co-investment opportunities  
- Re-underwriting annually  

### **3.3 Exit Planning**
- Redemption timelines  
- Market conditions  
- Distribution modeling
