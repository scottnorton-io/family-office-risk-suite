Each micro-guide is a 1-minute education block designed for spouses, children, staff, and elders.

---

## 1. Passwords
- Never reuse passwords.  
- Use a password manager.  
- Enable MFA everywhere.  
- Long > complex.  

---

## 2. Messaging
- Prefer Signal or iMessage.  
- Never trust unsolicited links.  
- Avoid WhatsApp cloud backup.  

---

## 3. Social Media
- No travel announcements.  
- No home background photos.  
- Avoid showing expensive assets.  
- Review tagged photos monthly.  

---

## 4. Email Safety
- Stop and read.  
- Look for urgency or pressure.  
- Never open attachments from unknown senders.  

---

## 5. Device Safety
- Update devices weekly.  
- Use strong passcodes.  
- No unknown charging cables.  

---

## 6. Home Safety
- Do not share access codes.  
- Avoid revealing schedules publicly.  
- Confirm visitors before opening doors.
