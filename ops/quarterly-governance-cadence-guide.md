# Quarterly Governance Cadence Guide  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## Purpose  
This guide defines the recurring quarterly rhythm that ensures the Family Office maintains alignment across:

- Strategy  
- Risk  
- Privacy  
- Wealth  
- Cyber posture  
- Vendor performance  
- Operational readiness  

---

# 1. Quarterly Breakdown

## **Quarterly Month 1 — Assessment & Intake**
- Update digital footprint  
- Device checks  
- Financial systems review  
- Vendor review (contracts + performance)  
- Operational risk reassessment  
- Privacy exposure recheck  
- Update family profiles (kids, health, travel, etc.)  

## **Quarterly Month 2 — Remediation & Optimization**
- Apply fixes from assessments  
- Implement security improvements  
- Patch vulnerabilities  
- Replace failing vendors  
- Update travel protocols  

## **Quarterly Month 3 — Reporting & Governance**
- Deliver Quarterly Risk Report  
- Deliver Quarterly Financial Summary  
- Deliver Vendor Oversight Summary  
- Deliver Threat Landscape Brief  
- Governance meeting  
- Update annual roadmaps
