## 1. Purpose  
To ensure all insurance claims are handled with precision, completeness, and legal defensibility.

---

## 2. Immediate Steps  
1. Stabilize safety  
2. Document scene (photos/video)  
3. Notify insurer  
4. Notify Family Office  
5. Preserve evidence  
6. Log every communication  

---

## 3. Required Documentation  
- Incident report  
- Police report (if applicable)  
- Witness statements  
- Receipts  
- Proof of ownership  
- Restoration invoices  

---

## 4. Audit Trail Requirements  
- Timestamped logs  
- Chain-of-custody  
- Insurer correspondence  
- Settlement offers  
- Final payout statement
