## 1. Account Compromise
- Change password  
- Revoke sessions  
- Enable MFA  
- Review forwarding rules  
- Check device logs  
- Freeze financial accounts if linked  

---

## 2. Device Compromise
- Disconnect from Wi-Fi  
- Do not power off  
- Notify cyber lead  
- Imaging/forensics  
- Provide backup device  

---

## 3. SIM-Swap
- Contact carrier  
- Lock SIM  
- Rotate passwords  
- Revalidate recovery numbers  

---

## 4. Lost Device
- Activate remote wipe  
- Freeze associated accounts  
- Replace device  
- Conduct exposure review
