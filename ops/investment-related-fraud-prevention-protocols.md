# Investment-Related Fraud Prevention Protocols  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Threats Covered  
- Wire fraud  
- Ponzi schemes  
- Misrepresentation  
- Fake investment offerings  
- Insider collusion  
- Phishing directing to fake custodians  

---

## 2. Controls  

### **Verification**
- Confirm any wire instructions verbally  
- Validate custodian details independently  
- Confirm any offering through a second advisor  

### **Custodian Security**
- MFA required  
- No email approvals  
- Daily transaction reviews  

### **Opportunity Vetting**
- Third-party review  
- Background checks on principals  
- Never invest under time pressure  

---

## 3. Escalation Path
- Staff → FO Director → Investment Lead → Principal
