# Contingency Communications Plan  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## Purpose
To ensure uninterrupted, secure communication during cybersecurity, privacy, or physical-security incidents affecting family members or the family office.

---

## 1. Communication Priority Levels
| Level | Objective | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **P1 – Life Safety** | Immediate emergency | Kidnapping threat |
| **P2 – Critical Operations** | Prevent damage | Account compromise |
| **P3 – Advisory** | Situational awareness | Travel alerts |

---

## 2. Primary Channels
- **Signal** (preferred)  
- **iMessage**  
- **ProtonMail**  

---

## 3. Backup Channels
- Satellite phone (where deployed)  
- Temporary travel phones  
- VPN-protected WhatsApp (if absolutely required)  

---

## 4. Emergency Words & Authentication

### **4.1 Green Word (All Good)**  
Used to confirm identity voluntarily.

### **4.2 Yellow Word (Distress Without Danger)**  
Indicates you're being watched or cannot speak freely.

### **4.3 Red Word (Immediate Threat)**  
Triggers escalation to EP and emergency services.

---

## 5. Silent Check-Ins
For minors, elders, or individuals traveling alone:
- Periodic "Alive & OK" pings  
- Non-response triggers location check  
- Repeated non-response triggers EP  

---

## 6. Device Failure Protocol
If a phone becomes unusable:
1. Attempt Signal from tablet/laptop  
2. Switch to travel phone  
3. Notify assigned contact  
4. Escalate to EP if location unknown
