# Client Discovery Interview Template  
*HNWI, SFO, and MFO Versions*  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## Purpose

This interview template standardizes the discovery process for new and prospective clients across HNWI, SFO, and MFO structures.  
It ensures:

- Consistency  
- Depth  
- Contextual relevance  
- Risk comprehension  
- Understanding of goals and sensitivities  

This template is used during onboarding, annual reviews, and when uncovering new needs or restructuring the client relationship.

---

# SECTION A — GENERAL QUESTIONS (ALL CLIENT TYPES)

## A1. Personal & Family Context  
- Tell me about your family structure.  
- How many individuals should be included in your protection scope?  
- Are there minor children, elderly parents, or dependents?  
- Are there household staff, nannies, drivers, or assistants?  
- Are there upcoming life events (marriage, divorce, inheritance, relocation)?

## A2. Financial & Professional Overview  
- What is your primary source of wealth?  
- How diversified are your assets?  
- Do you have operating companies, trusts, or foundations?  
- Do you serve on boards or advisory groups?  
- What advisors currently support you?

## A3. Current Pain Points  
- What brought you to us?  
- What’s your biggest frustration today?  
- What incidents have you faced in the last 12–24 months?  
- What worries you the most?

---

# SECTION B — CYBERSECURITY & TECHNOLOGY

## B1. Accounts & Identity  
- What email providers do you use?  
- Are you using MFA on all accounts?  
- Have you experienced identity theft before?  
- How many online accounts do you actively use?

## B2. Devices  
- What devices do you own?  
- Do you use separate devices for work and personal use?  
- Do you use travel-only devices?

## B3. Communication & Privacy  
- What messaging apps do you use?  
- Do you use any encrypted messaging services?  
- Do you share credentials within the family?

## B4. Data Exposure  
- Have you completed data broker removals before?  
- Have you been doxxed or exposed publicly?  
- Do you maintain public social media accounts?

---

# SECTION C — FINANCIAL ENVIRONMENT

## C1. Banking & Custodians  
- Which banks/custodians do you use?  
- Do any users have transactional authority?  
- Has anyone experienced account fraud?

## C2. Financial Apps  
- What portfolio reporting platforms do you use?  
- Do you use aggregators (e.g., Plaid)?  
- Do any vendors have standing access to your accounts?

---

# SECTION D — HOME & OPERATIONAL ENVIRONMENT

## D1. Home Setup  
- How many residences do you maintain?  
- Is there a home network administrator?  
- Are smart home devices used extensively?

## D2. Security  
- Do you employ physical security or executive protection?  
- Have you experienced trespassing or harassment?  
- Do you travel with security?

---

# SECTION E — TRAVEL & LIFESTYLE

## E1. Travel Patterns  
- How frequently do you travel?  
- Do you travel internationally?  
- Do you visit high-risk regions?

## E2. Exposure  
- Have you faced theft, scams, or border searches?  
- Does anyone announce travel publicly (e.g., Instagram)?

---

# SECTION F — HNWI-SPECIFIC QUESTIONS

- Do you participate in media interviews?  
- Do you maintain a public presence?  
- Are there any known stalkers, threats, or paparazzi incidents?  

---

# SECTION G — SFO-SPECIFIC QUESTIONS

- What is your governance maturity level?  
- How formal is your operating structure?  
- How many vendors do you rely on?  
- Do you have investment or legal teams in-house?  

---

# SECTION H — MFO-SPECIFIC QUESTIONS

- What is the size of the family pool?  
- What are the key operational bottlenecks?  
- What cross-family risks exist?  
- How standardized are your processes?  

---

# SECTION I — GOALS & OUTCOMES

- What does success look like?  
- How do you prefer to work with us?  
- What level of transparency do you want?  
- What is your tolerance for operational burden vs. security?
