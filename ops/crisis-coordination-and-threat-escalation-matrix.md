# Crisis Coordination + Threat Escalation Matrix  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Escalation Levels
| Level | Trigger | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **1** | Mild anomaly | Monitor |
| **2** | Confirmed exposure | Contain |
| **3** | Compromise | Lockdown |
| **4** | Safety threat | EP activation |
| **5** | Life-threatening | Full crisis team |

---

## 2. Command Structure
- Incident Commander  
- Cyber Lead  
- EP Lead  
- Communications Lead  
- Family Liaison  

---

## 3. Crisis Decision Tree
1. Is life safety impacted?  
   - Yes → EP Lead  
   - No → Cyber Lead  
2. Is data compromised?  
   - Yes → Containment  
3. Is family exposed publicly?  
   - Yes → Privacy Lead
