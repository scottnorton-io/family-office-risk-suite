## 2. Event Risk Actions  

### **Marriage**
- Prenuptial agreements  
- Asset segregation  
- Beneficiary updates  

### **Divorce**
- Entity ownership review  
- Revocation of POAs  
- Insurance adjustments  
- Address privacy lockdown  

### **Birth/Adoption**
- Add dependents to insurance  
- Update guardianship  
- Update trusts  

### **Death**
- Executor activation  
- Trust instructions  
- Asset freeze protocols  
- Litigation risk assessment
