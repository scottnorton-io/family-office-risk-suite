# Quarterly / Semi-Annual Report Template – Outline

This outline defines the structure for recurring review reports for family office clients.

It ensures a consistent, decision-ready format across HNWI, SFO, and MFO clients.

---

## 1. Snapshot Page

- **Period Covered:** (e.g., Q1 2026)  
- **Overall Risk Posture:** Simple visual (e.g., improved / stable / deteriorated).  
- **Key Messages:**
  - 3–5 bullet points summarizing the period.

---

## 2. Major Changes Since Last Review

- New vendors or platforms.  
- Changes in staff, advisors, or key roles.  
- New entities, jurisdictions, or significant life events.  
- Notable regulatory or legal developments affecting the client.

Keep explanations short and contextual.

---

## 3. Risk Register Updates

- **New Risks:** Summary of any new items added.  
- **Closed / Mitigated Risks:** Key items that were addressed.  
- **Top Open Risks:** Highlight 3–7 that matter most now.

Consider using small tables with:

- ID, title, change since last period, owner, status.

---

## 4. Vendor & WealthTech Updates

- **Key changes:** New vendors, retired vendors, major updates.  
- **Observations:** Anything noteworthy about performance or risk posture.  
- **Recommendations:** Any vendor-related decisions to consider.

For MFOs, this may include platform-level metrics and patterns.

---

## 5. Governance & Operating Model Updates

- Changes to roles, decision rights, or escalation paths.  
- Adjustments to the governance calendar or meeting cadence.  
- Summary of any workshops or education sessions.

Where applicable, note:

- “We clarified X”  
- “We reduced dependence on Y”  
- “We added a simple rule for Z”

---

## 6. Incidents & Near Misses

- Brief descriptions of any incidents or near misses during the period.  
- How they were handled (what went well, what was hard).  
- Lessons learned and any governance changes resulting from them.

Keep details appropriate to the privacy expectations of the client.

---

## 7. Recommended Decisions & Next Steps

This is the **most important section** for busy principals and leaders.

- **Decisions to Make:**  
  - Vendor changes?  
  - Additional controls?  
  - Governance adjustments?  

- **Actions to Take (Next 90 Days):**  
  - Short list of prioritized steps with owners.

- **Questions for Consideration:**  
  - Strategic questions the client or board should think about.

---

## 8. Appendix (Optional)

- Detailed risk register export (if needed).  
- Vendor inventory and changes.  
- Technical details or logs for those who want more depth.

This outline should be implemented in the chosen reporting format (deck, doc, secure portal) and kept consistent over time for each client.
