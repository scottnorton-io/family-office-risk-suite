## 2. Protection Requirements  

### **2.1 Physical Security**
- 24/7 monitoring  
- Climate control  
- Secure storage vaults  
- Verified transportation vendors  

### **2.2 Insurance Requirements**
- Scheduled items  
- Appraisal every 3 years  
- Photographic inventories  
- Specialized riders  

### **2.3 Custodial Standards**
- Provenance documentation  
- Chain-of-custody logs  
- Authenticity certificates  

---

## 3. Digital Records  
Store:
- Photos  
- Appraisal reports  
- Insurance policies  
- Condition reports  
- Ownership documentation  

In:
- Primary vault  
- Secondary encrypted backup
