# Daily Operations Manual (DOM) — Family Office Edition  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

# 1. Purpose  
This Daily Operations Manual (DOM) establishes the operational backbone of the Family Office.  
It ensures continuity, accountability, and a predictable rhythm of execution across:

- Wealth management  
- Risk & security  
- Privacy protection  
- Vendor operations  
- Communications  
- Governance  
- Travel logistics  
- Household operations (if applicable)

The DOM is designed for:  
- SFOs (1–20 staff)  
- MFOs (20–200 staff)  
- Embedded teams serving HNWI households  

---

# 2. Daily Cadence Overview  
The Family Office operates on a structured daily rhythm:

### **08:00 — Morning Systems Review**
- Account monitoring  
- Fraud alerts  
- Device alerts  
- Family calendar sync  
- Travel movement checks  
- Vendor ticket updates  

### **08:30 — Command Brief**
- Risk movements  
- Pending approvals  
- Operational blockers  
- Escalations from staff  

### **09:00–12:00 — Deep Work Block**
Examples:  
- Financial reconciliations  
- Vendor due diligence  
- Travel planning  
- Document preparation  
- Smart home or network updates  
- Data broker removals  
- Policy updates  

### **12:00 — Security & Privacy Sweep**
- Credential exposures  
- Smart home status  
- Email rules audit  
- Financial transaction review  

### **14:00 — Client/Family Interaction Window**
- Updates  
- Requests  
- Approvals  
- Travel briefings  

### **16:00 — End-of-Day Audit**
- Incident checks  
- Approval backlog  
- Vendor handoff  
- Documentation updates  
- Risk register deltas logged  

---

# 3. Weekly Operating Rhythm

### **Monday — Security & FinOps Sync**
- Account reviews  
- Cash flow projections  
- Cybersecurity review  

### **Wednesday — Vendor & Staff Review**
- MSP ticket audit  
- Advisory updates  
- Service performance  

### **Friday — Governance Wrap-Up**
- Risk register updates  
- Documentation refreshes  
- Next week planning  

---

# 4. Incident & Issue Management

### **Incident categories:**
- Cybersecurity  
- Privacy  
- Physical  
- Financial  
- Travel  
- Operational  

### **Every incident must include:**
- What happened  
- Who reported  
- Timelines  
- Root cause  
- Mitigation  
- Follow-up actions  
- Risk register update  

---

# 5. Documentation Standards
- Every client interaction recorded  
- Every approval logged  
- Every vendor ticket documented  
- Every risk movement captured  
- Changes must be timestamped  
- Only secure vaults permitted  
- No local-storage documents allowed  

---

# 6. Decision Records
Significant decisions require a Decision Record (DR):

- Topic  
- Options considered  
- Risks reviewed  
- Decision maker  
- Timestamp  
- Next review date
