## 1. Purpose  
This Charter defines the legal governance framework for HNWI, SFO, and MFO structures.  
It ensures clarity, accountability, and legal resilience across:

- Asset ownership  
- Trust governance  
- Entities & subsidiaries  
- Estate planning  
- Contracts  
- Liability management  
- Regulatory compliance  
- Cross-border operations  

---

## 2. Legal Governance Principles  
### **2.1 Preservation of Family Legacy**  
Ensure legal structures protect generational assets.

### **2.2 Reduced Exposure**  
Minimize litigation, liability, and compliance risks.

### **2.3 Transparency Within the Family Office**  
Roles and authorities must be clearly defined.

### **2.4 Confidentiality & Access Control**  
Legal assets must be protected using vault-based storage.

### **2.5 Annual Revalidation**  
All legal structures must be reassessed annually.

---

## 3. Legal Authority & Roles

### **Principal(s)**
- Own the overall legal mandate  
- Approve major legal changes  
- Set legal risk appetite  

### **Family Office Director**
- Operational oversight  
- Coordinates with attorneys  
- Manages access rights  

### **General Counsel / External Legal Team**
- Provide legal strategy  
- Review entity structures  
- Handle contracts, disputes, and negotiations  

### **Trustees**
- Fiduciary responsibility  
- Oversight of trust terms  
- Distributions and beneficiary management  

---

## 4. Decision Rights & Legal Boundaries  
| Area | Authority | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Entity formation | Principal + Counsel | Requires full documentation |
| Contract approval | Counsel + Director | Above threshold requires Principal |
| Litigation defense | Counsel-led | Principal must approve settlements |
| Trust amendments | Principal + Trustees | Dependent on trust type |

---

## 5. Legal Record Requirements  
All legal documents must be stored in a **primary encrypted vault**, including:

- Trust agreements  
- Operating agreements  
- Contracts  
- Estate plans  
- NDAs  
- Cross-border compliance certificates  

Documents must be version controlled, tagged, and access-restricted.

---

## 6. Annual Legal Review Components  
- Estate documents  
- Trust structures  
- Entity compliance  
- Tax residency  
- Beneficiary designations  
- Powers of attorney  
- Insurance adequacy  
- Cross-border operations  
- Open litigations or disputes
