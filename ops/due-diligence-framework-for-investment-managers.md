# Due Diligence Framework for Investment Managers  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Purpose  
A standardized due diligence methodology ensures investment managers meet performance, operational, compliance, and risk standards required by the Family Office.

---

## 2. DD Categories

### **2.1 Business & Organization**
- Firm ownership  
- Key personnel  
- Talent retention  
- Succession planning  

### **2.2 Performance & Strategy**
- Track record  
- Benchmark alignment  
- Strategy drift  
- Drawdown history  
- Risk-adjusted returns  

### **2.3 Risk Management**
- Portfolio construction  
- Hedging  
- Stress testing  
- Concentration limits  

### **2.4 Operational Due Diligence (ODD)**
- Compliance  
- Audit  
- Reporting accuracy  
- Cybersecurity posture  
- Vendor usage  
- Business continuity  

### **2.5 Fees & Terms**
- Management fees  
- Carried interest  
- Lock-ups  
- Redemption rights  
- Side letters  

---

## 3. Scoring Model  
Each category scored **1–5**, aggregated into:
- Performance risk  
- Operational risk  
- Strategy risk  
- Overall rating  

---

## 4. Required Documentation
- ADV filings  
- Audited financials  
- Compliance manual  
- Custodian details  
- Compliance breach history  
- Disaster recovery policies
