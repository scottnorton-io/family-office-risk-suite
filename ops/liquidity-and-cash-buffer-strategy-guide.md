# Liquidity & Cash Buffer Strategy Guide  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Purpose  
Ensures sufficient liquidity for:
- Lifestyle spending  
- Taxes  
- Emergencies  
- Investment commitments  
- Opportunistic investments  

---

## 2. Liquidity Tiers

### **Tier 1 — Immediate (0–30 days)**
- Checking  
- Treasury money markets  
- High-yield savings  

### **Tier 2 — Near-Term (30–180 days)**
- Short-duration bonds  
- Stable-value funds  

### **Tier 3 — Medium-Term (6–24 months)**
- Laddered bonds  
- Low-volatility alternatives  

---

## 3. Recommended Buffers  
- **Lifestyle:** 12–24 months  
- **Taxes:** 100% of next-year estimate  
- **Commitments:** Full capital call buffer  
- **Opportunistic:** Principal determined  

---

## 4. Monitoring  
- Monthly reconciliation  
- Quarterly scenario testing  
- Annual liquidity stress test
