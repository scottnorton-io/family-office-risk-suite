# Asset Protection & Liability Shielding Framework  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Purpose  
Protect the family’s wealth through legal, operational, and structural shielding techniques.

---

## 2. Protection Layers (Tiered Model)  

### **Tier 1 — Personal Protections**
- Umbrella insurance  
- Proper titling  
- Asset segregation  
- Homestead protections  

### **Tier 2 — Legal Structures**
- Trusts  
- LLC wrappers  
- Foundations  
- Corporate entities  

### **Tier 3 — Operational Controls**
- Contract risk review  
- Staff vetting  
- Vendor compliance  
- Secure communications  
- Document retention governance  

### **Tier 4 — Privacy Controls**
- Address suppression  
- Identity masking  
- Digital footprint reduction  

---

## 3. Risk Hotspots  
- Rental properties  
- Public businesses  
- Vehicles with drivers  
- Household staff interactions  
- Multi-state property ownership  
- Cross-border tax systems  

---

## 4. Annual Assessment  
- Structure review  
- Liability exposure scoring  
- Jurisdictional risk updates  
- Trust/LLC amendments if required
