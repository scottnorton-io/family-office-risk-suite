## 1. Purpose  
This guide establishes a monitoring system to detect deterioration, risk buildup, manager weakness, or structural issues in the portfolio.

---

## 2. Monitoring Frequency  
- Monthly: Performance + risk reports  
- Quarterly: Deep review  
- Annual: Full strategic realignment  

---

## 3. Key Indicators  

### 3.1 Performance-Related  
- Consecutive monthly underperformance  
- Drawdown exceeding strategy norms  
- Benchmark divergence  

### 3.2 Risk-Related  
- Increased volatility  
- Concentration spikes  
- Leverage changes  
- Style drift  

### 3.3 Operational  
- Staff turnover  
- Regulatory issues  
- Audit findings  
- Reporting inconsistencies  

### 3.4 Macro & External  
- Liquidity shifts  
- Regime changes  
- Contagion risk  
- Geopolitical instability  

---

## 4. Escalation Steps  
1. Identify indicator  
2. Validate with manager  
3. Investigate deeper  
4. Decide action (reduce, redeem, observe)  
5. Document decision
