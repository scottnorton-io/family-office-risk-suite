## 1. Purpose  
Sets retention periods for legal documents and defines secure destruction practices for physical and digital records.

---

## 2. Retention Schedule  

| Document Type | Retention |
|----------------|------------|
| Corporate records | Permanent |
| Trust documents | Permanent |
| Contracts | Term + 7 years |
| Litigation files | 10 years after closure |
| Compliance files | 7 years |
| Identity documents | Active use + 1 year |
| Travel docs | 2 years |

---

## 3. Destruction Requirements  
- Crypto-erase digital files  
- Cross-cut shred physical files  
- Maintain destruction logs  
- No disposal through household trash
