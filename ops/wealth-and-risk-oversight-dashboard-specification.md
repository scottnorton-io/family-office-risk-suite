# 1. Purpose  
Defines a central dashboard for Principals, Directors, and Analysts that unifies:

- Wealth position  
- Liquidity  
- Risk posture  
- Cyber alerts  
- Privacy exposure  
- Vendor status  
- Travel movement  
- Upcoming obligations  

---

# 2. Core Widgets

### **2.1 Risk Posture**
- Total risks  
- High risks  
- Critical risks  
- Mitigations overdue  

### **2.2 Wealth Summary**
- NAV  
- Cash availability  
- Debt exposure  
- Investment allocation  

### **2.3 Cyber Health**
- Devices healthy  
- Accounts protected  
- Exposed credentials  
- Smart home alerts  

### **2.4 Privacy Exposure**
- Active data brokers  
- Address exposures  
- Leaked credentials  

### **2.5 Vendor Health**
- Contracts expiring  
- Tickets open  
- SLA breaches  

### **2.6 Travel**
- Who is traveling  
- High-risk destinations  
- Check-in status
