## 1. Purpose

This guide establishes standards for redacting, handling, transferring, storing, and destroying sensitive documents.

---

## 2. Redaction Requirements

### 2.1 Redact:
- Full names  
- Signatures  
- Addresses  
- Banking details  
- Account numbers  
- Legal case numbers  
- Confidential clauses  

### 2.2 Redaction Rules
- Use *electronic redaction*, NOT black boxes.  
- Remove metadata.  
- Flatten PDFs after redaction.  
- Verify no hidden layers remain.  

---

## 3. Document Handling

- Use encrypted email only.  
- No forwarding of confidential files.  
- Store in secure vaults (1Password, Egnyte, NetDocuments).  
- No cloud sync to personal devices.  
- Delete local copies immediately after use.  

---

## 4. Document Destruction

- Secure erase digital copies.  
- Physical documents shredded cross-cut.  
- Validate deletion before confirming.
