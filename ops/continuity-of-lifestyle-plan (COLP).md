# Continuity-of-Lifestyle Plan (COLP)  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## Purpose
The COLP ensures that the family can maintain essential quality of life during disruptions — financial, travel, operational, reputational, or security-related.

---

## 1. Lifestyle Continuity Categories

### 1.1 Financial Continuity
- Secure cash channels  
- Emergency access to funds  
- Trusted financial delegate  

### 1.2 Communications Continuity
- Backup devices  
- Backup numbers  
- Secure channels  

### 1.3 Travel Continuity
- Backup passports  
- Verified hotels  
- Pre-cleared drivers  
- Medical access abroad  

### 1.4 Digital Continuity
- Cloud backup of essentials  
- Redundant access to key accounts  
- Offline access to documents  

### 1.5 Household Continuity
- Generator plans  
- Backup water  
- Critical supply list
