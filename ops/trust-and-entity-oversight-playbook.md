## 1. Overview  
This playbook establishes the management, risk oversight, and monitoring framework for:

- Trusts  
- LLCs  
- Holding companies  
- Foundations  
- Special-purpose vehicles (SPVs)  
- International entities  

---

## 2. Trust Oversight Responsibilities  

### **2.1 Trustees**
- Fiduciary compliance  
- Trust distributions  
- Tax reporting  
- Asset protections  
- Documentation upkeep  

### **2.2 Protector (if applicable)**
- Oversight of trustee behavior  
- Power to replace trustees  
- Safeguard family intent  

### **2.3 Family Office**
- Monitor trust operations  
- Ensure compliance with local law  
- Maintain trust documentation  
- Review distribution schedules  

---

## 3. Entity Oversight Framework  

### **Formation & Structure**
- Validate jurisdiction  
- Register entity  
- Document registered agent  
- Confirm ownership splits  

### **Operational Management**
- Accounts & banking  
- Licenses & permits  
- Insurance coverage  
- Board meeting minutes  
- Resolutions  

### **Compliance Calendar**
- Annual filings  
- Tax filings  
- Registered agent renewal  
- KYC/BSA reviews  

---

## 4. Trust/Entity Risk Indicators  
- Lawsuit exposure  
- Trustee conflict  
- Noncompliant distributions  
- Beneficiary disputes  
- Cross-border tax issues  
- Loss of residency status  

---

## 5. Monitoring Cadence  
| Frequency | Task |
|-----------|------|
| Monthly | Ownership or residency changes |
| Quarterly | Beneficiary updates |
| Semi-Annual | Compliance checks |
| Annual | Full structural review |
