# Insurance Governance & Coverage Architecture Guide  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Purpose  
This guide establishes insurance governance standards for HNWI, SFO, and MFO clients.  
Coverage must be comprehensive, overlapping where necessary, and aligned with:

- Lifestyle  
- Asset profiles  
- Cross-border risks  
- Liability exposures  
- Trust/entity structures

## 3. Coverage Adequacy Standards  

### Primary Requirements:
1. Umbrella minimum: **$20M** for HNWI (baseline), adjustable for net worth  
2. All properties insured to **replacement cost**, not market value  
3. All valuables scheduled individually  
4. Cyber coverage must include:
   - SIM-swap  
   - Digital extortion  
   - Identity remediation  
   - Social engineering  

---

## 4. Annual Review Checklist  
- Policy limits vs. net worth  
- Life changes (marriage, divorce, birth, death)  
- Property additions/removals  
- International travel frequency  
- Trust/entity restructuring  

---

## 5. Insurance Storage Requirements  
All policies must be stored in:
- Primary encrypted vault  
- Secondary backup vault  
- With renewal reminders set 30/60/90 days prior
