# Client Onboarding Guide  
*For HNWI, SFO, and MFO Clients*  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Welcome Message

Welcome to the Family Office Risk & Governance Suite.  
This onboarding guide provides an overview of the steps, expectations, timelines, and deliverables that will shape the next 30–90 days of your engagement.

Our objective is simple:  
**Provide world-class protection for your family, assets, privacy, and operations.**

---

## 2. Onboarding Timeline (30/60/90-Day Plan)

### **Day 1–10 (Kickoff Phase)**  
- Introductory meeting  
- Discovery interview  
- Access provisioning  
- Initial digital footprint sweep  
- Device baseline evaluation  
- Vendor and account inventory  

### **Day 10–30 (Assessment Phase)**  
- Full cybersecurity assessment  
- Privacy exposure analysis  
- Home network evaluation  
- Vendor due diligence  
- Documentation review  
- Travel risk profile creation  

### **Day 30–60 (Remediation Phase)**  
- Device hardening  
- MFA enrollment  
- Password manager activation  
- Data broker removals  
- Infrastructure segmentation  
- Smart home audit  
- Identity cleanup  

### **Day 60–90 (Governance Setup)**  
- Quarterly cadence establishment  
- Risk register handoff  
- Executive summary  
- Vendor oversight plan  
- Travel/incident response plan implementation

### Identity & Accounts
