## 1. Purpose  
To prepare the family and Family Office for potential legal disputes.

---

## 2. Litigation Triggers  
- Business disputes  
- Employee conflicts  
- Real estate issues  
- Breach of contract  
- Defamation  
- Fraud investigations  

---

## 3. Readiness Requirements  
- Maintain complete documentation  
- Control communications with opposing parties  
- Implement legal hold on documents  
- Notify relevant insurers  
- Restrict internal data access  
- Maintain discreet communication channels  

---

## 4. Standard Operating Procedure  
1. Incident reported  
2. Counsel notified  
3. Preservation protocol activated  
4. Legal strategy meeting scheduled  
5. Document production organized  
6. Communications controlled
