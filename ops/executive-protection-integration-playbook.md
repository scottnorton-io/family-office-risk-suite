## Overview
This playbook defines how Family Office risk, cyber, and privacy operations integrate with Executive Protection (EP) teams for a unified security posture.

---

## 1. Shared Objectives
- Protect life and safety  
- Prevent predictable patterns  
- Detect surveillance early  
- Respond to emergencies rapidly  

---

## 2. Coordination Points

### Pre-Travel
- Flight details  
- Hotel OSINT check  
- Local threat intelligence  
- Digital hygiene checklist  

### During Travel
- Secure communications  
- Real-time itinerary adjustments  
- Anti-surveillance movement patterns  

### Post-Travel
- Device scans  
- Exposure evaluation  
- Update risk register  

---

## 3. Threat Escalation
EP leads for:
- Physical threats  
- Stalking  
- Kidnap attempts  
- Residence breaches  

Cyber team leads for:
- Account compromise  
- Data exposure  
- Device infection
