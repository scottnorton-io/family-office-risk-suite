## 1. Medical Emergencies
- Call local emergency services  
- Notify designated medical coordinator  
- Provide medical binder (digital or physical)  
- Notify family office command  

---

## 2. Residence Emergencies
### Fire  
- Evacuate immediately  
- Meet at predetermined safe point  
- Notify EP  

### Break-In  
- Hide or evacuate based on plan  
- Trigger alarm  
- Contact EP before police to avoid escalation errors  

---

## 3. Travel Emergencies

### Loss of Passport  
- Contact embassy  
- Notify family office  
- Provide scanned ID from secure vault  

### Travel Scam or Theft  
- Disable cards  
- Freeze accounts  
- Inform travel security concierge  

---

## 4. Kidnap/Extortion Scenario
- Do not negotiate  
- Use emergency words  
- EP takes command  
- Foil social engineering attempts
