## 1. Purpose  
Ensures Family Office operations meet global anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-customer (KYC) requirements.

---

## 2. KYC Requirements  
### **2.1 Identity Verification**
- Government ID  
- Proof of address  
- Source of funds  
- Source of wealth  

### **2.2 Ongoing Monitoring**
- Sanctions list  
- Adverse media  
- Beneficial ownership changes  
- Politically Exposed Persons (PEP) status  

---

## 3. AML Controls  
- Transaction monitoring  
- Fraud detection  
- Suspicious activity escalation  
- Geographic risk scoring  
- Vendor AML certifications  

---

## 4. Annual Review  
- Update identity documentation  
- Review beneficial ownership  
- Reevaluate PEP status  
- Refresh sanctions screening
