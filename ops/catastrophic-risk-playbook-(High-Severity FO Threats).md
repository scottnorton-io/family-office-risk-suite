# Catastrophic Risk Playbook  
**Version 1.0 — Complete Document**

---

## 1. Purpose  
This playbook defines emergency procedures for high-severity scenarios relevant to ultra–high-net-worth individuals and family offices.

---

## 2. Catastrophic Risk Categories  

### **2.1 Personal / Safety**
- Kidnapping  
- Extortion  
- Hostage scenarios  
- Violent threats  
- Stalking escalations  

### **2.2 Cyber / Digital**
- Full account compromise  
- Compromise of financial systems  
- Identity takeover at scale  
- Blackmail leveraging private data  

### **2.3 Operational**
- Natural disasters impacting residences  
- Total data-loss scenarios  
- Infrastructure collapse  
- Vendor failure (custodian, MSP, concierge)  

### **2.4 Reputational**
- Media scandal  
- Leaked communications  
- Social media attack campaigns  

---

## 3. Command Structure  
### **Incident Commander**  
Family Office Director or Principal’s designate.  
### **Response Units:**  
- Cyber Lead  
- Executive Protection  
- Legal Counsel  
- Communications Lead  
- Medical Lead (if relevant)  

---

## 4. Crisis Actions (Standard 7-Step Protocol)  

1. **Stabilize Safety**  
2. **Secure Communications**  
3. **Isolate Affected Systems**  
4. **Engage Relevant Specialists**  
5. **Document Everything**  
6. **Communicate Only Through Secure Channels**  
7. **Initiate Long-Term Damage Control**

---

## 5. Required Tools  
- Secure messaging  
- Satellite phone (optional)  
- Travel emergency binder  
- Identity freeze kits  
- K&R emergency line
