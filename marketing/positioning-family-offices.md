# Positioning: Family Office Risk & Governance

## Who We Serve

- Single-family offices (SFOs)
- Multi-family offices (MFOs)
- High-net-worth families operating through advisors

Especially those with:

- Complex financial and personal lives,
- Significant reliance on third-party vendors,
- Limited internal cyber/compliance capacity, and
- A strong preference for discretion and simplicity.

without a trusted, independent view of their true exposure.
