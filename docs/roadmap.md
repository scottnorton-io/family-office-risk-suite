# Family Office Offering Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Define & Design (0–4 weeks)

- [ ] Finalize service catalog (this repo).
- [ ] Define 3–6 concrete packages with indicative pricing.
- [ ] Draft positioning copy for family offices and referral partners.
- [ ] Write a one-page overview suitable for PDF and website.

**Exit criteria:**  
We have a clear, internally consistent offer that can be explained in
5 minutes or less to a family office principal or advisor.

---

## Phase 2 – Assets & Collateral (3–8 weeks)

- [ ] Create content for:
  - [ ] Landing page / website section
  - [ ] One-page PDF overview
  - [ ] "Family Office Risk Briefing" slide deck
- [ ] Draft at least 3–5 thought-leadership pieces.
- [ ] Create partner pack for wealth managers / attorneys / CPAs.

**Exit criteria:**  
We have enough collateral to support warm intros, events, and follow-ups
without improvising every time.

---

## Phase 3 – Go-To-Market Experiments (6–16 weeks)

- [ ] Define ideal client profile (ICP) and specific target lists.
- [ ] Pilot 1–3 micro-roundtables or briefings with trusted partners.
- [ ] Run initial discovery calls with early adopters.
- [ ] Close 1–3 pilot clients on clear, bounded packages.

**Exit criteria:**  
We have validated demand, pricing ranges, and messaging with real buyers.

---

## Phase 4 – Operationalization & Scale

- [ ] Turn deliverables into repeatable templates and playbooks.
- [ ] Define roles and capacity limits for delivery.
- [ ] Refine pricing and packaging based on pilot feedback.
- [ ] Decide whether to treat this as:
  - A standalone practice line, or
  - A flagship offering under a broader advisory umbrella.
